Rabies Vaccine Development

Pasteur's Rabies Discovery

After studying rabies virus, Pasteur developed the first successful vaccine against the disease - a major breakthrough in medicine!

The Breakthrough:

By using weakened virus samples from infected animals, Pasteur created a vaccine that stimulated immunity without causing the disease.
